About us

CFI is the technical society of JGEC. It has been promoting technical knowledge of the students since its establishment (2010). Along with conduction of classes, CFI also deals with projects and provides adequate support to carry out the same. In short, CFI is an organisation where students come-in together to uplift their technical know-how - for the betterment of themselves and for the society.
