we are

CFI is the technical society of JGEC. It has been promoting technical knowledge of the students since its establishment (2010). Along with conduction of classes, CFI also deals with projects and provides adequate support to carry out the same. In short, CFI is an organisation where students come-in together to uplift their technical know-how - for the betterment of themselves and for the society.

SRISTI

Largest Techno Management Fest of North Bengal

Every time we hear SRISTI, our mind and heart roar out in unison — YOU ARE HERE TO IMAGINE, YOU ARE HERE TO CREATE! For all those daydreamers out there, with the remotest of ideas ranging from a new opening in chess to a new use of an Arduino, you must know that if you are passionate about it, these turn into an obsession.

SRISTI is the grand annual techno-management gala of Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College which was ﬁrst started in 201 0, the largest of its kind in North Bengal. JGEC has been at the forefront of technical expertise ever since its inception in 1961; SRISTI was conceived much later in, and it's among the best of the feathers in our hat, helping us to further stamp our authority with aplomb. With its {{ sristi_date }}th year approaching, we know better than how it has helped us climb the ladder ofsuccess one rung at a time and we welcome you all to be a part of it.

So, gear up for this mega event where you'll have to compete with passionate and like—minded individuals. From propelling a rocket to debating your heart out in GMUN, from trying to be the Quizzard to modelling a bridge till dawn, we shall be with you all the time. And it's a given that you will be cheered as much for a goal in Robo—Soccer or FIFA as for a headshot in Counter—Strike.The 4 days might ignite a spark in the ever-curious brain of yours and you'll enjoy the process. It's our way of life! Make it yours too.

And as Donovan Livingston says — "The sky is not the limit, its just the beginning! Lift Off.”
